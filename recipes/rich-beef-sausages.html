Now that's what I call rich beef sausages!

Sausage? I hardly know her!

Sausages have been around for at least 20 years, however cutting edge research suggests this may be as long as 23! You can't spell 'feed' without 3 of the letters found in 'Rich Beef Sausages'! And if that's good enough for me, you better believe it is good enough for you!

Ingredients

  • Richard
  • Cow
  • Sausage casings
  • 1 BonBonBonBon

Steps

  1. Get your Richard/Rich (preferably of the Madely variety) and chuck that sucker in a pan!
  2. Turn your Cow into Beef and add to the pan
  3. Take the forsaken mixture and stuff it into a casing
  4. Garnish with Bonbonbonbon and enjoy!

Congratulations! You've got rich beef sausages!
